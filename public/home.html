Hi Alex, Welcome to StudBud!


StudBud is..

a Study application with the tools you'll need to get through your studies

Key Features

Task Board

Keep track off all your tasks. List them by priority to make sure you can prioritise the tasks that are more important!

Virtual Study Space


Who doesn't like their own personal space where they are in control of their surroundings?


Dive your head into your personal study space listening to your favourite tunes and having a task tracker to count your progress in completing your tasks!

Additional Features


Dictionary


Feel like you're sometimes unsure of what different words mean? We've got you covered. When you're deep in your studies, click on the book for access to your personal dictionary

Music Player

Who doesn't like listening to a tune when they are studying. Play your favourite playlist straight from StudBud without having to connect to an external music player or platform.
